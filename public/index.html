How Hoy works

A quick guide for guests and hosts.

1
Find a place

On Homes, search by city and pick your dates and number of guests. Tap any listing to see photos, what's included, the house rules, and the host's profile and reviews.

2
Book it

Some listings are Instant Book — they confirm right away. Others are Request to book, where the host has 24 hours to accept. You pay securely by card when you book, and Hoy holds the money safely until your stay.

3
Message your host

A private chat opens once your booking is confirmed (or once the host accepts your request). That's where you share your arrival time and ask questions. Messaging stays closed before then — it keeps everyone safe and on Hoy.

4
Experiences & Services

Same idea: pick an experience (a tour, a meal, a class) or a service (chef, photographer, henna, tutoring, cleaning), choose a date and how many people, and book. You meet the host at the spot, or they come to you.

5
Check in & enjoy

We send a reminder 24 hours before. Your check-in details show up in your chat with the host.

6
Leave a review

After your stay or service, you can review the host. Reviews come only from people who actually booked, so they're real.

7
Changes & cancellations

Free cancellation within 24 hours of booking. After that, refunds follow the cancellation policy shown at checkout.

1
List it

Add your home, experience, or service. Set your price, add photos straight from your camera or gallery, set your house rules, and choose Instant Book or Request to book.

2
Get requests

For request-to-book, you have 24 hours to accept or decline. Accepting opens a private chat with your guest so you can sort out the details.

3
Host them

Welcome your guest, share check-in details in the chat, and deliver a great stay, experience, or service.

💳 How you get paid

The guest pays by card the moment they book. Hoy holds that money safely in escrow — this protects you and the guest. You're then paid out to your EVC / mobile money once the booking is complete:

🏠 Homes & hotels24 hours after the guest checks in
🎈 Experiences & services24 hours after it's completed

This 24-hour buffer is the same model Airbnb uses — it leaves room to sort out any check-in problem before the money is released. Hoy keeps a 3% host service fee; the rest is yours. You can see exactly what you'll earn in your Earnings tab.

4
Build your reputation

Guest reviews build your star rating and bring more bookings. Accepting quickly and keeping cancellations low keeps you in good standing.

5
If you must cancel

The guest is fully refunded. Frequent last-minute host cancellations can pause your listings, so try to only confirm dates you can keep.

Verify your identity

Upload a valid photo ID. We accept a passport, Somali National ID (NIRA), foreign government ID, driver's license or residence permit. This earns your "Verified" badge — the thing that makes a guest comfortable booking you.

How you'll get paid · required

Hoy pays hosts via EVC Plus after each completed stay. Your payout goes to the exact number below, so it must be a live EVC Plus account that can receive money.

Please add your name, upload a photo ID, enter an EVC Plus number, confirm it can receive money, and sign all policies to continue.

Host Payout Policy

Please read this before you sign. Checking the box on the form is your electronic signature.

1. How you're paid. Hoy holds each guest's card payment securely after they book. Once the stay or experience is completed, Hoy releases your payout to the EVC Plus number on your host account, minus Hoy's service fee.

2. Your account is your responsibility. You must give a correct EVC Plus number and account name, and you must keep that account active, registered, and able to receive money at all times. If your number changes, you must update it before your next payout. Hoy is not responsible for funds sent to a number you provided that is wrong, inactive, or unable to receive money.

3. Three payout attempts. Hoy will make up to three (3) attempts to send each payout to your registered EVC Plus number. If all three attempts fail — for example because the account is inactive, unregistered, blocked, over its limit, or the number is incorrect — Hoy will stop trying. The funds are not lost: they are held until you contact Hoy with a valid, working EVC Plus account. Repeated payout failures may result in your listings being paused or removed.

4. Timing and escrow. Payouts are released after the guest's stay or experience is complete. By default, a payout releases automatically about 24 hours after checkout, unless a guest dispute or safety review is open — in which case Hoy may hold it until the matter is resolved.

5. Fees. Hoy deducts its service fee from each booking before payout. You receive the net amount shown in your dashboard.

6. Limits and compliance. Payouts are subject to EVC Plus / Hormuud limits and to applicable anti-money-laundering and verification rules. Hoy may delay or withhold a payout where needed to verify identity or investigate suspected fraud.

7. Third-party services. EVC Plus is operated by Hormuud, not Hoy. Hoy is not responsible for delays, outages, fees, or limits set by EVC Plus or any mobile-money provider.

8. Taxes. You are responsible for any taxes owed on your earnings.

9. Changes. Hoy may update this policy. Continuing to host after an update means you accept the changes.

10. Electronic signature. Accepting this policy and creating your host account is your binding electronic signature, recorded with the date and time.
